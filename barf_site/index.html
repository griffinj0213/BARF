Build Art for Renn Fayre

Renn Fayre doesn't just happen, you have to build it. This site will hopefully give you some ideas of what and how you can make Renn Fayre projects. Renn Fayre is a the-more-the-better situation, so everyone should try their hand at making some art! You don't have to be good at it, you just have to do it.
